About

Growing up I LOVED videogames but all i had was my PC and no gaming consoles, but I had a library full with 80s-90s videogames from sega genesis and nintendo 64 and other consoles,
there was a single game that was my favorite, it was a party game with multiple mini games, you can play it couch co-op or against the computer and it can take up to 4 players.
The game had Sherlock Holmes world style, and it was ammmmmaaazzzing
Unfortunately the game was in japanese only and i don't know the name of it, and not sure what was the console.
Once i knew that i had to make a Features project for Holberton school, i knew it was going to be a mini-clone of my favorite mini game of it, which is maze solving.
